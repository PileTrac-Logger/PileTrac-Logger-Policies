Introduction

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Pile Stroke Log (referred to as the "App"), created by the developer. The App is a utility tool designed to measure the blow rate and stroke height of pile-driving hammers using real-time audio analysis.

We believe in complete transparency and user privacy. Because Pile Stroke Log operates entirely locally on your mobile device, we do not collect, process, transmit, or store any personal data.

Permissions & Usage

To function correctly, the App requires specific system permissions on your mobile device. Here is why they are needed and how they are used:

  • Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO): Used to stream audio input for real-time blow rate and stroke height analysis. We do not record, save, or transmit any audio files. All sound analysis happens entirely in-memory and is discarded immediately after processing.
  • Storage Access (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Used to allow you to save and export generated hammer stroke log reports as PDF or CSV files to your device's storage (e.g., Downloads folder).

No Collection or Transmission of Information

When you use the App, we do not gather any information, personal or technical. Specifically:

  • No registration, login, or accounts are required.
  • No name, email address, or device identifier is collected.
  • No location data (GPS or IP-based) is requested or tracked.
  • No automated crash reporting or analytical SDKs are integrated.

Note on Sharing: If you choose to share your log reports (PDF or CSV) using the Share functionality within the App, this is initiated solely by your manual action and utilizes the standard Android system share sheet to send the file to your designated target (e.g., Email, Messaging apps).

Children's Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal data or transmit any information, it does not knowingly collect or request information from children under the age of 13.
