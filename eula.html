1. Description of Service

The App is a tool designed to assist in monitoring pile driving operations by using audio analysis to estimate hammer impact timing and calculate the stroke height. The App allows the user to input project data, record blow counts, and generate PDF reports.

2.1 Technical Limitations:

 

The App relies on microphone input and acoustic signatures to detect hammer impacts. By using the App, you acknowledge that the accuracy of the App's assessments can be significantly affected by environmental noise, hardware quality, atmospheric conditions, and physical distance from the hammer.

2.2 Not a Primary Measurement Tool:

 

The data provided by the App is for informational and supplemental purposes only. Any and all data produced by the App should not be used as the primary or sole method for determining pile capacity, stroke height, or structural integrity.

2.3 Verification Required:

All measurements generated by the App should be independently verified using physical markings (i.e., foot-interval marks) or manufacturer-certified instrumentation.
